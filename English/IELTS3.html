Describe something challenging you achieved recently. You should say: - What it was - Why it was challenging - How you achieved it

نمونه پاسخ (60 ثانیه):

"I recently completed my first 5K run, which was quite challenging as I'd never been a runner before. The main difficulty was building stamina - I could barely run for 5 minutes when I started.

I followed a gradual training plan: • Started with walk-run intervals • Increased distance weekly • Practiced 3 times per week • Used a fitness app to track progress

The achievement felt incredible because I pushed past my comfort zone and proved to myself that consistent effort pays off."
