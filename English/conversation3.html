Restaurant & Food Ordering

Server:
Welcome to Golden Plate. Have you dined with us before?
به گُلدن پِلِیت خوش آمدید. قبلاً پیش ما غذا خورده‌اید؟
Customer:
No, this is my first time. Could you recommend any house specialties?
نه، اولین باره. میتونید غذاهای مخصوص رستوران رو پیشنهاد کنید؟
Server:
Our chef's signature dish is the grilled salmon with herb sauce. Also, the mushroom risotto is quite popular.
غذای مخصوص سرآشپز ما سالمون گریل شده با سس سبزیجات است. همچنین، ریزوتو قارچ هم خیلی محبوبه.
Customer:
I'll go with the salmon. How spicy is it? I'm a bit sensitive to very spicy food.
سالمون رو انتخاب می‌کنم. چقدر تنده؟ من یکم به غذاهای خیلی تند حساسم.
Server:
It's very mild, but we can adjust the seasoning to your preference. Would you like to start with any appetizers?
خیلی ملایمه، ولی می‌تونیم چاشنی رو طبق سلیقه شما تنظیم کنیم. مایلید با پیش غذا شروع کنید؟

Restaurant Vocabulary & Phrases

• house specialty / signature dish
غذای مخصوص رستوران
• mild / medium / spicy
ملایم / متوسط / تند
• adjust the seasoning
تنظیم کردن چاشنی
• appetizer / main course / dessert
پیش غذا / غذای اصلی / دسر
• to your preference/taste
طبق سلیقه شما

Useful Restaurant Phrases

Making Reservations:
• I'd like to make a reservation for...
• Do you have a table for... people?
• What time do you have available?

Ordering Food:
• I'll have the...
• How would you recommend this be cooked?
• Does this contain any allergens?

Special Requests:
• Could I have this without...
• Is it possible to substitute...
• Could you make it less spicy?

Cultural Tips

• Always inform about allergies
• It's okay to ask about ingredients
• Tipping customs vary by country
• Don't be afraid to ask for recommendations
• Learn local dining etiquette
