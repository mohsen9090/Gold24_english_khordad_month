English.Online24 | Slang & Idioms Lesson 2

Level: B1-C1

Emotions & Reactions

Lose your cool
To become angry or upset
از کوره در رفتن، عصبانی شدن
Don't lose your cool over small problems.
Over the moon
Extremely happy or delighted
خیلی خوشحال، در پوست خود نگنجیدن
She was over the moon when she got accepted to university!
Butterflies in your stomach
Feeling nervous or excited
دلشوره داشتن، هیجان‌زده بودن
I had butterflies in my stomach before the job interview.
Face like thunder
Looking very angry
قیافه عصبانی، اخم‌آلود
When he came out of the meeting, he had a face like thunder.

Modern Emotional Expressions

I can't even
Unable to handle a situation emotionally
نمی‌تونم تحمل کنم، از دست رفتم
That movie was so sad, I can't even!
Shook
Shocked or surprised
شوکه شده، متحیر
When I heard the news, I was completely shook.
Big mood
Strongly relating to something
کاملاً درک می‌کنم، دقیقاً همینطور
"I just want to sleep all day." "Big mood!"

Common Reactions in Text

Emotional Shortcuts
• OMG = Oh my god (خدای من)
• IKR = I know, right? (می‌دونم، درسته؟)
• TFW = That feeling when (اون حسی که)
• NGL = Not gonna lie (صادقانه)
• IMO = In my opinion (به نظر من)
