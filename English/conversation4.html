Shopping & Negotiating Prices

Customer:
Excuse me, I'm interested in this leather jacket. Could you tell me the price?
ببخشید، من به این کت چرم علاقه‌مند شدم. میشه قیمتش رو بگید؟
Salesperson:
This is one of our premium items. It's $299, but we're having a 20% discount this week.
این یکی از اقلام ممتاز ماست. قیمتش ۲۹۹ دلاره، ولی این هفته ۲۰٪ تخفیف داریم.
Customer:
I see. And do you have it in a medium size? Also, is it genuine leather?
متوجه شدم. و سایز متوسطش رو دارید؟ همچنین، آیا چرم اصل هست؟
Salesperson:
Yes, we have it in medium. It's 100% genuine leather with a one-year warranty. Would you like to try it on?
بله، سایز متوسط داریم. ۱۰۰٪ چرم اصل هست با یک سال گارانتی. مایلید پرو کنید؟
Customer:
Yes, please. And what's your return policy if it doesn't fit well?
بله، لطفاً. و سیاست مرجوعی شما چیه اگر اندازه مناسب نباشه؟

Essential Shopping Expressions

Asking About Products:
• Do you have this in...? (size/color)
• What's it made of?
• Is this the latest model?

Negotiating Price:
• Could you give me a better price?
• Is that your best price?
• What's the final price with discount?

Payment & Returns:
• Do you accept credit cards?
• Can I get a receipt?
• What's the exchange/return period?

Shopping Tips

• Always check return policies
• Ask about warranties/guarantees
• Compare prices before buying
• Keep receipts for returns
• Check for seasonal sales
• Be polite when negotiating
