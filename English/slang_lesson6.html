American Pop Culture & Movie Slang

Basic
Mainstream, unoriginal
عادی، تکراری
Drinking pumpkin spice latte is so basic.
Salty
Upset, angry, bitter
ناراحت، عصبانی
Don't be salty just because you lost the game.
Tea
Gossip, drama
شایعه، اخبار جنجالی
What's the tea on the new couple?

Movie & TV Show Slang

Plot twist
Unexpected turn of events
اتفاق غیرمنتظره
Plot twist: he was the villain all along!
Binge-watch
Watch multiple episodes continuously
پشت سر هم سریال دیدن
I binge-watched the entire season last night.
