سوال 4:

Modern lifestyles mean that many parents have little time to spend with their children. What are the causes of this situation? What effects could this have on children and society?

پاسخ نمونه (برای سوال 4):

Introduction:
The issue of limited parent-child interaction in modern society is becoming increasingly concerning, with various causes and potentially serious consequences.

Body Paragraph 1 - Causes:
• Long working hours and demanding careers
• Extended commute times in urban areas
• Increased reliance on digital devices
• Economic pressures requiring both parents to work

Body Paragraph 2 - Effects on Children:
• Reduced emotional support and guidance
• Potential behavioral issues
• Academic performance might suffer
• Increased dependence on electronic entertainment

Body Paragraph 3 - Societal Impact:
• Weakening of family bonds
• Increased social isolation
• Higher stress levels in families
• Long-term psychological effects

Conclusion:
While modern lifestyles have created this challenge, society needs to find ways to balance work demands with family time to ensure healthy child development and social stability.
