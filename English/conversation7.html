Business Meetings & Presentations

Presenter:
Good morning everyone. Today I'll be presenting our quarterly sales report and future projections.
صبح بخیر همگی. امروز گزارش فروش سه ماهه و پیش‌بینی‌های آینده رو ارائه می‌کنم.
Manager:
Before you start, could you share the presentation slides with everyone?
قبل از شروع، می‌تونید اسلایدهای ارائه رو با همه به اشتراک بذارید؟
Presenter:
Of course. I've just shared them via email. As you can see from the first graph, our sales have increased by 25% since last quarter.
البته. همین الان از طریق ایمیل به اشتراک گذاشتم. همونطور که در نمودار اول می‌بینید، فروش ما از سه ماه گذشته ۲۵٪ افزایش داشته.
Participant:
Could you elaborate on how we achieved this growth? What were the main contributing factors?
می‌تونید درباره چگونگی دستیابی به این رشد توضیح بدید؟ عوامل اصلی چی بودن؟

Business Meeting Vocabulary

• quarterly report
گزارش سه ماهه
• sales projections
پیش‌بینی‌های فروش
• contributing factors
عوامل موثر
• market share
سهم بازار

Presentation Phrases

Opening:
• Let me begin by...
• Today's agenda covers...
• I'd like to outline...

During Presentation:
• Moving on to the next point...
• As you can see from this chart...
• Let me highlight...

Handling Questions:
• That's an excellent question
• Let me clarify that point
• I'll look into that and get back to you

Business Email Phrases

• I am writing to inform you...
• Please find attached...
• As discussed in our meeting...
• I look forward to your response
• Best regards
