Common Daily Expressions

Break a leg!
Used to wish someone good luck (especially in performing arts)
موفق باشی! (به خصوص در هنرهای نمایشی)
My daughter has an audition today. I told her to break a leg!
It's piece of cake
Something that is very easy to do
خیلی آسونه، مثل آب خوردن
Don't worry about the test. It's a piece of cake!
Hit the hay
To go to bed or go to sleep
رفتن به رختخواب، خوابیدن
I'm exhausted. I think I'll hit the hay early tonight.
Spill the beans
To reveal a secret
لو دادن یک راز
Come on, spill the beans! What did he say about me?

Situations & Usage

Casual Conversations:
• With friends and family
• In informal settings
• On social media

When to Avoid:
• Formal meetings
• Professional emails
• Academic writing

Modern Variations

No cap
No lie, telling the truth
بدون دروغ، حقیقت محض
No cap, that was the best movie I've ever seen!
Slay
To do something exceptionally well
عالی انجام دادن کاری
Your presentation today? You totally slayed it!
