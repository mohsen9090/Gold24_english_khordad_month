Perfect Tenses & Reporting Structures

ساختارهای Perfect پیشرفته

1. Present Perfect + just/already/yet
2. Past Perfect + Reporting Verbs
3. Perfect Infinitives (to have done)

کاربردهای پیشرفته

• She claimed to have seen the incident.
(او ادعا کرد که حادثه را دیده است)
• By the time we arrived, they had already left.
(وقتی ما رسیدیم، آنها قبلاً رفته بودند)
• The CEO is reported to have signed the contract.
(گفته می‌شود که مدیرعامل قرارداد را امضا کرده است)

تمرین‌های تخصصی

    2. 1. "I have completed the project" → She said _______
    2. "We had been working all night" → They reported _______
    3. "The market has improved" → The analysts claimed _______
