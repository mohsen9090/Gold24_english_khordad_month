Travel & Hotel Booking

Guest:
Hi, I'd like to check the availability for a double room next weekend.
سلام، می‌خواستم موجود بودن یک اتاق دو نفره برای آخر هفته آینده رو چک کنم.
Receptionist:
Certainly. What dates were you looking at specifically?
حتماً. دقیقاً چه تاریخ‌هایی مد نظرتون هست؟
Guest:
From Friday the 15th to Sunday the 17th. And I was wondering if you offer airport shuttle service?
از جمعه ۱۵ام تا یکشنبه ۱۷ام. و می‌خواستم بدونم آیا سرویس رفت و برگشت فرودگاه دارید؟
Receptionist:
Yes, we have rooms available for those dates. Our deluxe double room is $180 per night, including breakfast and free WiFi. The airport shuttle runs every two hours from 6 AM to 10 PM.
بله، برای اون تاریخ‌ها اتاق داریم. اتاق دو نفره لوکس ما شبی ۱۸۰ دلاره، شامل صبحانه و وای‌فای رایگان. سرویس فرودگاه هر دو ساعت از ۶ صبح تا ۱۰ شب کار می‌کنه.
Guest:
Perfect. And what's your cancellation policy? Just in case my plans change.
عالیه. و سیاست کنسلی شما چیه؟ برای احتیاط اگر برنامه‌ام تغییر کرد.
Receptionist:
You can cancel free of charge up to 48 hours before check-in. Would you like to proceed with the booking?
تا ۴۸ ساعت قبل از ورود می‌تونید بدون هزینه کنسل کنید. مایلید رزرو رو انجام بدیم؟

Common Hotel Requests

Room Service:
• Could I order room service?
• I'd like to request housekeeping
• Can I get an extra pillow/towel?

Facilities:
• What time does the gym/pool open?
• How do I connect to the WiFi?
• Where is the business center?

Special Requests:
• Can I get a late check-out?
• Is it possible to change rooms?
• Do you have any quiet rooms?

Travel Tips

• Always confirm check-in/out times
• Save hotel address in local language
• Take photos of room condition on arrival
• Keep booking confirmation handy
• Ask about included services
• Note emergency numbers & exits

Room Types Guide

• Single Room: 1 person
• Double Room: 2 people, one bed
• Twin Room: 2 people, separate beds
• Suite: Multiple rooms with living area
• Deluxe: Premium room with extra amenities
