Internet Culture & Meme Language 🌐

It's giving...
Reminds me of/looks like
شبیه... هست، یاد... میندازه
Your outfit? It's giving main character energy!
Living for this
Really enjoying or appreciating something
عاشق این چیزم، خیلی خوشم میاد
Your new hair? I'm living for this!
