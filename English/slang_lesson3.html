Level: B1-C1

Internet & Social Media Slang

Spill the tea
Share the gossip or drama
خبر جدید رو تعریف کردن، غیبت کردن
Come on, spill the tea about what happened at the party!
Living rent free
Something you can't stop thinking about
چیزی که از ذهن بیرون نمیره
That song is living rent free in my head.
Main character energy
Acting like you're the most important person
رفتار کردن مثل شخصیت اصلی، خود مهم پنداری
She walked into the room with main character energy.

Popular Abbreviations

• IYKYK = If you know, you know (اگه می‌دونی، می‌دونی)
• TBH = To be honest (صادقانه بگم)
• FOMO = Fear of missing out (ترس از دست دادن)
• GOAT = Greatest of all time (بهترین همه دوران)
• IMO/IMHO = In my (humble) opinion (به نظر من)

Trending Expressions

It's giving...
It reminds me of/looks like
شبیه... هست، یاد... میندازه
That outfit is giving summer vibes.
Based
Being yourself/having a strong opinion
محکم و قاطع بودن در عقیده
That's a pretty based opinion, not gonna lie.
