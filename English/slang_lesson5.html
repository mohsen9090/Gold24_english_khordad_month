American Street Slang & Everyday Expressions

Hit me up
Contact me, call me, text me
با من تماس بگیر، بهم پیام بده
Hit me up when you're free to hang out.
What's good?
How are you? What's up?
چطوری؟ چه خبر؟
Yo, what's good? Haven't seen you in a minute!
Bet
Okay, sure, agreed
باشه، حله، موافقم
"Want to grab lunch?" "Bet!"

Popular American Expressions

Cap/No Cap
Lie/No lie, truth
دروغ/راست، حقیقت
No cap, this is the best pizza in town.
On God
I swear, honestly
قسم می‌خورم، به خدا
On God, I didn't take your phone!
