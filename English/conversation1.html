Making Plans for the Weekend

Sarah:
Hey Tom, do you have any plans for this weekend?
سلام تام، برای این آخر هفته برنامه‌ای داری؟
Tom:
Not really. I was thinking about catching up on some work, but I'm open to suggestions.
نه واقعاً. داشتم فکر می‌کردم کمی به کارهام برسم، ولی برای پیشنهادات آماده‌ام.
Sarah:
Well, there's this new art exhibition downtown. I heard it's really worth checking out.
خب، یه نمایشگاه هنری جدید تو مرکز شهر هست. شنیدم واقعاً ارزش دیدن داره.
Tom:
That sounds interesting! What kind of art is being featured?
به نظر جالب میاد! چه نوع هنری نمایش داده میشه؟
Sarah:
It's a mix of contemporary and traditional pieces. They've got paintings, sculptures, and even some interactive installations.
ترکیبی از آثار معاصر و سنتیه. نقاشی، مجسمه و حتی چندتا چیدمان تعاملی دارن.

Key Vocabulary & Expressions

• catching up on (something)
رسیدگی کردن به (چیزی)
• open to suggestions
پذیرای پیشنهادات
• worth checking out
ارزش دیدن/بررسی داشتن
• being featured
به نمایش گذاشته شدن
• interactive installations
چیدمان‌های تعاملی

Practice Questions

1. How would you invite a friend to do something this weekend?
2. What's your response when someone suggests an activity you're interested in?
3. How do you describe an event that you think others would enjoy?
4. Practice using "worth + verb-ing" in different contexts.
5. Create a similar dialogue about making plans for a different activity.
