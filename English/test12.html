آزمون زبان انگلیسی - درس 12

1. Indirect Questions:

Could you tell me _____ the train station is?

2. Would Rather:

I _____ stay home tonight _____ go to the party.

3. Expressing Purpose:

She went to the bank _____ some money.

4. Making Suggestions:

Why don't we _____ to the beach tomorrow?

5. Past Habits:

When I was a child, I _____ spend hours reading comics.

6. Both/Neither/Either:

_____ of the restaurants are open today.

7. Expressing Agreement:

"I don't like spicy food." "_____ do I."

8. Unless/If:

_____ you study harder, you won't pass the exam.

9. Expressing Preference:

I _____ coffee _____ tea.

10. Reported Commands:

The teacher told us _____ during the test.
